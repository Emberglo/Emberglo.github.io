About

Ryan has always been an inquisitive person which drew him to the natural sciences. He spent six years studying Wildlife Biology at Boise State University. He worked in retail management for 10 years before deciding that he needed to look for a change. He wanted to find something that could satisfy his curiousity, createtivity, and desire to challenge himself. When he found software development, he knew he had found something that would satisfy all three.

Software Developer

Ryan attended Boise CodeWorks to learn Full-Stack Software Development

Ryan has experience using HTML, CSS, Sass, Bootstrap, Tailwind, JavaScript, jQuery, and VueJS to create beautiful and functional front-end user experiences.
On the back-end, Ryan has used NodeJS with Express and MongoDB with Mongoose to structure the back-ends of his applications and to build his own servers.
Ryan also uses Git with Github as version control for his projects. He also has experience incorporating libraries to extend the functionality and appearance of his application.
